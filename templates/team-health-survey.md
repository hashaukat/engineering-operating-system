# Team Health Survey

> Run anonymously every quarter. The same questions every time, so trends are visible. Results are owned by the EM, shared with the team unredacted, and rolled into the [scorecard](../docs/metrics-scorecard.md). See [productivity measurement](../docs/productivity-measurement.md) for the theory.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Team | `<team name>` |
| Quarter | `Q<n> YYYY` |
| Respondents | `<n> / <team size>` |
| EM | `<name>` |
| Anonymity | Anonymous; results shared in aggregate |

---

## Instructions for respondents

- Answer honestly. Anonymity is real.
- Use a 1–5 scale: **1 = strongly disagree, 5 = strongly agree.**
- "N/A" is allowed and useful — it tells us a question doesn't apply.
- The free-text fields are the most valuable part. Please use them.

---

## Section 1 — DX Core 4

1. **Speed** — *I can ship a small change to production in less than a day.*
2. **Effectiveness** — *I am working on the things that matter most.*
3. **Quality** — *I trust the code I am asked to change.*
4. **Impact** — *My work matters to customers.*

## Section 2 — Flow & focus

5. *I have enough uninterrupted time to do deep work.*
6. *Meetings are a reasonable share of my week.*
7. *I am rarely blocked for more than half a day.*
8. *Our on-call rotation is sustainable for me.*

## Section 3 — Direction & autonomy

9. *I understand our team's quarterly outcomes and why they matter.*
10. *I have the autonomy to do my job well.*
11. *When I disagree with a decision, I have a real way to be heard.*
12. *Our team makes decisions at the right level — not too centralized, not too fragmented.*

## Section 4 — Growth & support

13. *I am growing in this role.*
14. *I get useful feedback on my work.*
15. *My manager is invested in my development.*
16. *I see a path forward at this company (IC or management track).*

## Section 5 — AI-augmented work *(specific to an AI-first org)*

17. *I have the AI tools I need to do my best work.*
18. *I feel safe to reject an AI suggestion when I think it's wrong.*
19. *AI assistance has made my work more enjoyable.*
20. *I understand our policy on AI usage and can apply it confidently.*

## Section 6 — Team & belonging

21. *I trust the people I work with.*
22. *Our team handles disagreement well.*
23. *I can bring my full self to work.*
24. *I would recommend this team to a friend.*

## Section 7 — Free text

- One thing this team should keep doing:
- One thing this team should stop doing:
- One thing this team should start doing:
- The biggest blocker to my productivity right now is:
- Anything you want to tell leadership that is not on this survey:

## How results are used

- **EM** reviews aggregate results within 1 week.
- **Team retro** within 2 weeks; team chooses 1–2 improvement actions.
- **Director** reviews trends across teams; flags org-level patterns.
- **Scorecard:** the average of Q1 (DX Core 4) and Q4 (sustainability) feed the [Leverage lens](../docs/metrics-scorecard.md).
- **Trend** — any question dropping > 0.5 points quarter-over-quarter is named and addressed.

## What we do not do with results

- We do not share individual responses.
- We do not punish low scores. Low scores are a *gift* — they tell us where to invest.
- We do not "fix" scores by re-wording the question. We re-word only after explicit team agreement.
